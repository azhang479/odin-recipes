Chicken Nuggets

What are Chicken Nuggets?

Chicken nuggets are bite-sized pieces of chicken that are breaded and fried until crispy and golden brown. A favorite among kids and adults alike, they can be made with either whole chicken breast pieces or ground chicken. Homemade nuggets are easy to make and often healthier than fast food versions. They're delicious when served hot with dipping sauces like ketchup, honey mustard, or barbecue sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (panko or regular)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Optional: your favorite dipping sauce

Instructions

  1. Set up three bowls: one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with breadcrumbs mixed with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Coat each piece of chicken in flour, then dip into the egg, and finally coat with the breadcrumb mixture.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Fry the nuggets in batches until golden brown and fully cooked, about 3â€“4 minutes per side.
  5. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
